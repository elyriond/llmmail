## ðŸ“¸ Essential Components for Professional Fashion Prompts

### 1. Photography Style Foundation
**Always start with a clear style declaration:**
- "Editorial fashion photography" (Vogue, Harper's Bazaar style)
- "Commercial fashion photography" (catalog, e-commerce)
- "Street style fashion photography" (candid, urban)
- "High fashion editorial" (avant-garde, artistic)
- "Lookbook photography" (clean, product-focused)

### 2. Technical Camera Specifications
Include specific technical details for photorealism:

**Camera Body & Lens:**
- "Shot on Canon EOS R5 with 85mm f/1.4 lens"
- "Hasselblad medium format, 110mm f/2.8"
- "Leica M10 with 50mm f/1.4 Summilux"
- "Sony A7R IV with 70-200mm f/2.8 at f/2.8"
- "Phase One XF IQ4 150MP, 80mm f/2.8 lens"

**Technical Settings:**
- "Shallow depth of field, f/1.4, bokeh background"
- "8K resolution, ultra high detail"
- "4K photorealistic quality"
- "Shot at ISO 100 for maximum clarity"
- "1/125 shutter speed, capturing movement"

### 3. Lighting Descriptions (CRITICAL)
Lighting makes or breaks fashion photography:

**Studio Lighting:**
- "Soft diffused studio lighting with large octabox"
- "Dramatic Rembrandt lighting with hard key light"
- "Clamshell lighting setup for beauty shot"
- "Ring light for even, shadowless illumination"
- "Three-point lighting with rim light separation"
- "Butterfly lighting creating shadow under nose"

**Natural Light:**
- "Golden hour sunlight, warm directional light"
- "Overcast diffused natural light, soft shadows"
- "Window light from camera left, soft and flattering"
- "Sunset backlight creating rim glow"
- "Bright midday sun with fill reflector"

**Creative/Mood Lighting:**
- "Neon street lights, cyberpunk color palette"
- "Warm candlelight, intimate atmosphere"
- "Spotlight creating dramatic vignette"
- "Colored gel lighting, magenta and cyan contrast"
- "Volumetric fog with backlight rays"

### 4. Composition & Framing
Be specific about camera angle and framing:

**Shot Types:**
- "Full-length portrait, head to toe in frame"
- "Three-quarter length shot, waist up"
- "Close-up portrait, shoulders and face"
- "Wide environmental shot showing location"
- "Detail shot focusing on fabric texture"

**Camera Angles:**
- "Eye-level perspective, straight on"
- "Low angle looking up, powerful stance"
- "High angle looking down, editorial style"
- "Dutch angle for dynamic tension"
- "Over-the-shoulder perspective"

**Rule of Thirds & Composition:**
- "Centered composition, symmetrical balance"
- "Subject positioned on rule of thirds intersection"
- "Negative space on left, subject right-aligned"
- "Leading lines drawing eye to subject"
- "Frame within frame composition"

### 5. Fashion-Specific Details

**Clothing Description:**
- Be ULTRA specific about garments, fabrics, colors
- "Black silk charmeuse slip dress with cowl neckline"
- "Oversized camel wool coat, minimalist tailoring"
- "Emerald green velvet blazer with peak lapels"
- "Distressed denim with frayed hems and patches"
- "Ivory lace bodysuit with scalloped edges"

**Texture & Material:**
- "Flowing chiffon catching the breeze"
- "Structured leather with visible grain"
- "Soft cashmere knit with cable details"
- "Metallic lamÃ© with liquid shine"
- "Matte crepe with precise draping"

**Styling Elements:**
- "Minimalist gold jewelry, delicate chains"
- "Bold statement earrings, geometric shapes"
- "Vintage accessories, 1970s inspired"
- "Sleek pulled-back hairstyle, clean silhouette"
- "Textured braids with natural volume"

### 6. Setting & Environment

**Studio Settings:**
- "Seamless white backdrop, infinite background"
- "Textured concrete wall, industrial aesthetic"
- "Draped fabric background, soft folds"
- "Geometric panels, architectural backdrop"

**Location Settings:**
- "Urban rooftop at sunset, city skyline background"
- "Minimalist modern interior, floor-to-ceiling windows"
- "Cobblestone European street, old architecture"
- "Desert landscape, minimal natural environment"
- "Art gallery with white walls and spotlights"

### 7. Mood & Atmosphere
Add emotional context:
- "Confident and powerful energy"
- "Dreamy and ethereal mood"
- "Edgy and rebellious attitude"
- "Sophisticated and timeless elegance"
- "Playful and energetic vibe"

### 8. Color Palette
Specify color relationships:
- "Monochromatic black and white with gray tones"
- "Complementary color scheme: blue and orange"
- "Muted earth tones: beige, terracotta, olive"
- "Vibrant jewel tones: sapphire, emerald, ruby"
- "Pastel color palette: blush, mint, lavender"

## ðŸ’¡ Pro Tips for DALL-E 3

1. **Reference Real Photographers:** "Inspired by Annie Leibovitz" or "Peter Lindbergh style"
2. **Magazine Source Prompts:** "From Vogue editorial" or "Harper's Bazaar cover shoot"
3. **Avoid Faces When Possible:** Use "masked," "viewed from behind," or "face obscured" for fewer distortions
4. **Emphasize Quality:** Always include "8K," "ultra high detail," "photorealistic"
5. **Layer Details:** 3-5 sentences covering all aspects creates best results
6. **Consistent Brand Aesthetic:** Match lighting and mood to brand identity
